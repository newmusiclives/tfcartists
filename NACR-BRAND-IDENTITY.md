## üñºÔ∏è IMAGERY STYLE

### Photography Guidelines

**Subject Matter:**
- Open roads and wide horizons
- Acoustic instruments (guitars, banjos, harmonicas, fiddles)
- Hands playing instruments or writing lyrics
- Textures: wood grain, denim, leather, dust
- Golden hour and sunset lighting
- Campfires and twilight vibes
- Vintage Americana: diners, gas stations, motels, barn venues
- Wide-open landscapes: deserts, plains, mountains

**Mood:**
- Warm, nostalgic, but not overly sentimental
- Authentic, lived-in, real people
- Cinematic but not overly stylized
- Intimate moments + expansive vistas

**Color Treatment:**
- Warm tones emphasized (oranges, reds, browns)
- Slightly desaturated for vintage feel
- Golden hour preferred lighting
- Deep shadows for depth and mood

**Avoid:**
- Stock photo feel (overly posed, fake smiles)
- Cold or clinical lighting
- Urban/city settings (unless culturally relevant)
- Overly polished or airbrushed aesthetics

### Illustration Style (Optional)

**When to Use Illustrations:**
- DJ avatar graphics
- Show identity cards
- Merch designs
- Social media templates

**Style Guidelines:**
- Hand-drawn or woodcut-inspired
- Limited color palette (2-3 brand colors)
- Textured, organic line work
- Mix of vintage Americana + modern minimalism

## üåê WEB & APP DESIGN PRINCIPLES

### Layout
- **Generous white space** ‚Äî Let content breathe
- **Card-based design** ‚Äî Modular, organized, scannable
- **Sticky player** ‚Äî Music controls always accessible
- **Minimal navigation** ‚Äî Don't overwhelm

### Interactions
- **Smooth transitions** ‚Äî Fade-ins, gentle easing
- **Hover states** ‚Äî Subtle color shifts, no aggressive effects
- **Loading states** ‚Äî Thoughtful, on-brand (e.g., "Tuning in...")
- **Error states** ‚Äî Friendly, helpful, human

### Mobile-First
- Design for mobile first, scale up to desktop
- Touch targets minimum 44√ó44px
- Readable text without zooming (16px minimum body)
- Fast-loading images (WebP, lazy loading)

### Accessibility
- WCAG AA compliance minimum
- Color contrast ratios: 4.5:1 for body text, 3:1 for large text
- Keyboard navigation support
- Screen reader friendly (ARIA labels, semantic HTML)
