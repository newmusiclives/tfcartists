## The Origin Story

### The Problem (2020)

Paul Saunders watched hundreds of talented artists perform at small venues, pour their hearts out, and walk away with $50 from the door. He saw the disconnect:
- Fans WANTED to support artists
- Artists NEEDED the money
- But the friction was too high: "I don't have cash," "I'll Venmo you later" (they never did)

**The question:** How do you make it as easy to support an artist as it is to stream their music?

### The Solution (2021)

**Step 1: TrueFans CONNECT (The Donation Platform)**

Paul built TrueFans CONNECT — a simple website where fans can instantly donate to artists during live shows. One 9-word line from the stage:

> *"Go To True Fans CONNECT dot com Right Now!"*

That's it. No QR codes, no apps, no complexity.

**Step 2: TrueFans RADIO Network (The Distribution)**

Artists needed more than just live show donations. They needed:
- Airplay
- Discovery
- Consistent revenue between shows

So Paul built **TrueFans RADIO Network** — a network of internet radio stations where:
- Artists get FREE airplay
- Artists earn from a revenue pool funded by sponsors
- Listeners discover new music
- Everyone wins

**Step 3: North Country Radio (The Flagship)**

To prove the model, Paul launched **North Country Radio** (NACR) — the first TrueFans RADIO station, focused on americana, roots, and folk music.

## The TrueFans Ecosystem

### 1. New Music Lives (Parent Organization)
**Mission:** Help independent artists make a living from their music.

**Philosophy:**
- Artists shouldn't have to choose between art and paying rent
- Fans want to support artists, just make it easy
- Radio should help artists, not just play their music

### 2. TrueFans CONNECT (Donation Platform)
**Website:** TrueFansCONNECT.com

**How It Works:**
- Artist says the 9-word line at their show
- Fans visit TrueFansCONNECT.com on their phones
- They donate instantly ($5, $10, $20, whatever they want)
- Artist gets 80%, TrueFans gets 20%
- Money hits artist's account same night

**Why It Works:**
- No friction (just a website)
- Instant gratification (artist sees donations in real-time)
- Fair split (80/20 is better than most platforms)
- No setup required (just say the line)

### 3. TrueFans RADIO Network (Radio Stations)
**First Station:** North Country Radio (NACR)

**Business Model:**
- Local businesses sponsor the station
- 80% of sponsor revenue → Artist Revenue Pool
- 20% of sponsor revenue → Station operations
- Artists get FREE airplay
- Artists earn monthly from the revenue pool (based on airplay shares)

**Why It Works:**
- Artists get airplay AND money
- Sponsors support local artists (great story)
- Listeners discover new music
- Everyone benefits

### 4. North Country Radio (Flagship Station)
**Format:** Americana, roots, folk, singer-songwriter

**What Makes It Different:**
- Every artist on rotation is part of TrueFans CONNECT
- DJs mention how to support artists
- Revenue goes back to artists
- Community-focused, mission-driven

## Paul's Philosophy

### "Artists First"
Everything Paul builds starts with one question: **Does this help artists make money?**

If the answer is no, he doesn't build it.

### "Stay Close to the Mission"
Paul still hosts **The Long Road Home** every Saturday, 10am–2pm. He could hire someone else, but he doesn't. Why?

> *"If I'm not willing to DJ at 10am Saturday, I have no business asking other DJs to do it. The mission is the music. If I'm not listening, why should anyone else?"*

### "Simple Beats Complex"
The 9-word line works because it's simple. No app to download, no QR code to scan, no account to create. Just a website.

> *"The easier you make it, the more people do it. We're not trying to be clever. We're trying to help artists pay rent."*

### "80/20 Is Sacred"
TrueFans takes 20%, artists get 80%. Always.

> *"We could take 30% and artists would still use us. But that's not why we exist. We exist to help artists, not extract from them."*

## The TrueFans Model (Summary)

### For Artists:
1. **Say the 9-word line at shows** → Fans donate via TrueFansCONNECT.com
2. **Get FREE airplay on TrueFans RADIO** → No cost to join
3. **Earn from the revenue pool** → Monthly payouts based on airplay
4. **Keep 80% of everything** → Fair split, instant payouts

### For Sponsors:
1. **Support local artists** → Great story for your business
2. **Reach music fans** → Engaged, loyal audience
3. **Affordable packages** → $100-$500/month
4. **Measurable impact** → Monthly reports showing your contribution

### For Listeners:
1. **Discover new artists** → Curated by real DJs, not algorithms
2. **Support artists directly** → TrueFansCONNECT.com donations
3. **Community-driven** → Local artists, local sponsors, local impact
4. **FREE to listen** → No subscriptions, no ads (sponsors cover it)
