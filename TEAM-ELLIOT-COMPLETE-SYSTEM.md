## üèóÔ∏è HABIT FORMATION SYSTEM

Orion's framework for turning casual listeners into habitual listeners:

### The Habit Loop

```
TRIGGER ‚Üí ROUTINE ‚Üí REWARD
```

**Example Loop 1: Morning Commute**
- **Trigger:** Get in car at 7:30am
- **Routine:** Turn on TrueFans RADIO (Hank's show)
- **Reward:** Feel prepared, connected, inspired for the day

**Example Loop 2: Evening Wind-Down**
- **Trigger:** Finish dinner at 8pm
- **Routine:** Turn on Midnight Rodeo
- **Reward:** Feel relaxed, nostalgic, ready for sleep

**Example Loop 3: Work Focus**
- **Trigger:** Start work at 9am
- **Routine:** Background Americana playlist
- **Reward:** Feel calm, focused, productive

### Habit-Building Tactics

**Week 1:** Discovery
- Welcome email
- "Here's what you'll love about us"
- Sample playlists for different moods
- Goal: Get 1 full session

**Week 2-4:** Activation
- "Tune in today at 7am for [artist]"
- "You listened twice this week - here's what you missed"
- "Your favorite DJ is live now"
- Goal: Get to 3 sessions/week

**Month 2-3:** Retention
- Weekly "Your Week in Music" recaps
- Streak tracking ("12 days in a row!")
- Personalized recommendations
- Goal: Maintain 3+ sessions/week

**Month 4+:** Loyalty
- Community invitation
- "Listener of the Week" nomination
- Referral opportunities
- Goal: Turn into evangelist

### Notification Strategy

**Trigger Types:**
1. **Time-based:** "Your 7am show starts in 10 minutes"
2. **Event-based:** "Your favorite artist just went live"
3. **Habit-based:** "You usually listen around now"
4. **Social-based:** "23 people are listening right now"
5. **FOMO-based:** "You missed an amazing track yesterday"

**Frequency:**
- New listeners: 1-2 notifications/day
- Active listeners: 3-4 notifications/week
- Power users: 1-2 notifications/week (less is more)
- At-risk listeners: 2-3 reactivation messages over 2 weeks
